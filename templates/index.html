About DinnerDash

Dinner Dash is a web application done by students staying at in Cinnamon College. This application is targeted at operaters of the Cinnamon-Tembusu Dining Hall. It helps to estimate the amount of traffic at specific timings to allow the operater to better estimate when and how much food to prepare in that time frame. In essence, it helps to prevent scenarios where insufficient quantity of food prepared by the operator or having leftover food.

Try It Out!

Background

During dinner service, we often see that there either insufficient food prepared or there will a ton of leftover food that is thrown. Therefore, our aim is to curb this.

Solution

We came up with this web application that uses MACHINE LEARNING to help predict the expected number of people that are dining at specific times.
